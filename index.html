品牌简介

DGJHDUT

由香港知名时尚大师CAVIN和KALAN在2006年香港创立，携手香港凯文国际时尚有限公司倾力打造的都市时尚轻奢品 牌，品牌包含男女包袋、鞋履、皮带、太阳眼镜、首饰等各类时尚产品。品牌创始之初专为高瑞人群个性定制，因设计 时尚简约并富有现代都市感的独特风格，品质堪称精品，价格更加亲民，深受时尚人士欢迎，一直供不应求。品牌紧随 国际时尚潮流，融合设计师的创作灵感，演绎现代时尚潮流的魅力，已被视为潮流的象征，快时尚的标杆，赢得了众多 业内人士的关注和时尚一族的推崇，拥有大批忠实的追随者，跃身成为国内外炙手可热的轻奢品牌。现逐步在国内外开 设品牌体验店和扩展网络代理销售渠道，让每个人都能够拥抱时尚经典。

CAVIN & KALAN (Little CK for short) was founded by renowned fashion masters CAVIN and KALAN in 2006. It cooperates with Hong Kong Cavin International Fashion Co., Ltd. to create an urban fashion light-luxury brand, including various kinds of fashion products, such as men and women bags, shoes, belts and sun glasses and jewelries, etc. The brand follows the trend of international fashion, combines the creative inspiration of designers and expresses the charm of modern fashion trend. It has been regarded as the symbol of the trend and the benchmark of fast fashion, and won the attention of numerous industry personage and high praise of fashion master friends, with a large number of loyal followers. At present, it has become a hot light luxurious brand at home and abroad and gradually established the domestic and foreign brand experience stores and online sales channels, thus making everyone embrace the fashion classics.
